# uuid-ossp (uuid-ossp)

Level: Beginner+
Available locally: Yes

## One-line purpose

Generate UUIDs (versions 1, 3, 4, and 5) inside PostgreSQL, including time-based and namespace-based variants.

---

Content coming in a future stage.

Note: PostgreSQL 13+ includes `gen_random_uuid()` as a built-in (version 4). `uuid-ossp` is needed for v1/v3/v5 or for pre-PG13 compatibility.
